Application Process

Please follow the steps below to complete your application for admission to S.P. Memorial English Medium School.

1

Download the Application Form

Download and print the official application form using the button below.

Download Application Form

2

Fill Out the Form

Complete all sections of the application form clearly and accurately. Please use BLOCK LETTERS and blue/black ink only.

Important: Incomplete forms may delay the application process.

3

Gather Required Documents

Attach the following documents with your application form:

  • Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  • 2 recent passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card/Utility bill)

4

Submit Your Application

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents to the school's admission office during working hours:
